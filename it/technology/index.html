Privacy-First Architecture

Tutta l'elaborazione dell'analisi avviene localmente sul tuo dispositivo—nessun archiviazione cloud, nessuna raccolta dati.

Unlike traditional cloud-based analytics platforms, AnalyticsZone applications process all your training data locally on your device. This means:

  • Your workout data never leaves your device
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No third-party data sharing or selling
  • Works completely offline after initial download
  • You maintain complete ownership of your data

100% Local Processing

All analytics computations happen on your device using modern web technologies.

Peer-Reviewed Research

Every metric and calculation is based on published scientific studies and validated methodologies.

Scientifically-Backed Metrics

Costruito su ricerca peer-reviewed e metodologie di allenamento validate da scienziati dello sport leader.

Our applications implement training zone models and performance metrics from leading sports scientists:

  • Training Zones: Coggan, Daniels, Seiler methodologies
  • Performance Metrics: VO2max, lactate threshold, running economy
  • Training Load: TSS, TRIMP, training stress balance
  • Periodization: Evidence-based training phase planning
  • Race Prediction: Riegel and Cameron models

Open Standards & Compatibility

Compatibile con formati file fitness standard (FIT, TCX, GPX) da tutte le principali piattaforme.

Import your training data from any platform:

  • FIT Files: Garmin, Wahoo, Polar, Suunto
  • TCX Files: Training Center XML format
  • GPX Files: GPS Exchange Format
  • CSV Export: Universal spreadsheet compatibility
