Why We Built This

We tried every analytics platform out there. They were either too simple (just basic stats) or too opaque (magic AI scores with no explanation). And every single one wanted to upload our training data to their servers.

As data nerds, we wanted the formulas. As athletes, we wanted actionable insights. As privacy advocates, we wanted local-only processing.

So we built what we couldn't find: Apps that show the science, explain the calculations, and respect your privacy.

If you're the kind of person who wants to know why your VO₂max is 52, how TSS is calculated, and where the training zone formulas come from—welcome home.
